🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026  ·  Effective immediately upon registration

ThiranMitra ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform at localhost:5000 (development) or thiranmitra.in (production). By using ThiranMitra, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

👤
Account Data:

Name, email address, phone number, password (hashed with bcrypt), date of birth, gender, state, city.

💼
Career Data:

Education history, work experience, skills, certifications, job preferences, resume content you paste or upload, interview scores, aptitude test scores.

🔍
Usage Data:

Jobs viewed, saved, and applied to; schemes browsed; pages visited; search queries; chatbot interactions.

📱
Device & Technical Data:

IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers (for security and analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • ✅ Provide AI-powered job matching, resume analysis, skill gap reports, and interview training
  • ✅ Personalize your dashboard with career predictions and recommendations
  • ✅ Send job alerts and government scheme notifications (if opted in)
  • ✅ Improve our algorithms and platform security
  • ✅ Comply with legal obligations under Indian law (IT Act 2000, DPDP Act 2023)
  • ❌ We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT share your data with employers without your explicit consent to apply

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a SQLite database (thiranmitra.db) on our server. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text. Authentication uses JWT tokens with configurable expiry. In production, we recommend HTTPS with TLS 1.3 encryption. We retain your data as long as your account is active; you may request deletion at any time.

4. Your Rights (Under DPDP Act 2023)

📋
Right to Access
Request a copy of your data anytime
✏️
Right to Correct
Update incorrect information via Profile
🗑️
Right to Erase
Request account & data deletion
🚫
Right to Restrict
Opt out of marketing & alerts

5. Cookies & Local Storage

ThiranMitra uses localStorage (not cookies) to store your JWT authentication token (wm_token) and user data (wm_user) on your browser. This data is never shared with third parties. You can clear it by logging out or clearing browser storage.

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries, data deletion requests, or concerns, contact us at:
privacy@thiranmitra.in
We respond within 5 business days as required by DPDP Act 2023.
