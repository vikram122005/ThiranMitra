Bridging the Employment Gap

ThiranMitra (रोजगार सेतु) literally means "Employment Bridge" — our mission is to connect every Indian youth to opportunity, regardless of their background, language, or location.

Our Mission

Every Indian Deserves a Fair Shot at a Great Career

India has 65% of its population under 35, yet 23% of youth remain unemployed or underemployed. The gap isn't talent — it's access. Access to information, skill training, interview preparation, and quality job opportunities.

ThiranMitra is built to be that bridge — using AI to democratize career development for every youth in India, whether they're from a metro city or a remote village.

The Problem

What India's Job Seekers Face

😰

Information Gap

Millions of youth don't know which skills are in demand, which government schemes they qualify for, or how to prepare for interviews.

🌾

Rural Exclusion

65% of Indians live in rural areas, yet most job portals are designed for English-speaking urban users with internet and computer access.

💼

Skill-Job Mismatch

Employers report 60% of fresher hires aren't job-ready. Students get degrees but don't know what skills employers actually need in 2026.

Our Solution

How ThiranMitra is Different

🤖
AI-First Approach

Every feature is powered by AI — from resume scoring to career prediction to mock interview evaluation.

🌾
Rural Inclusive Design

Regional language support, low-bandwidth mode, and local job matching within 5km of any village.

🏛️
Government Integration

All 50+ central and state skill schemes, job alerts, and competitive exam notifications — all in one dashboard.

👩‍💼
Inclusive for All
