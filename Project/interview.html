⚙️ Interview Setup

✅ Your AI Interview Coach will analyze your answers for confidence, clarity, structure (STAR method), grammar, and relevance. You'll get a readiness score from 0–100% at the end!

📖 Interview Answer Patterns

⭐ STAR Method
Situation — Set the context
Task — Your responsibility
Action — What you did
Result — Quantified outcome
🎯 CARE Method
Concept — Core idea
Application — Real example
Result — Outcome achieved
Evaluation — What you learned
⚠️ Common Interview Mistakes to Avoid
❌ Giving vague, generic answers
❌ Not asking questions at the end
❌ Saying "I don't have weaknesses"
❌ Talking negatively about past employer
❌ Lack of specific examples
❌ Speaking too fast / too slow
✅ Pro Tips from Successful Candidates
  • Research the company thoroughly — mention specific products, recent news
  • Quantify everything: "Reduced costs by ₹2 lakh" beats "saved money"
  • Practice answered using STAR for all behavioral questions
  • Arrive 10 minutes early; for online interviews test tech 30 min before
  • Always send a follow-up thank-you email within 24 hours
